Adam Bell

Adam Bell is a student technologist, programmer, and community contributor based in Portlaoise, County Laois, Ireland. His work and public profile focus on software development, robotics, STEM education, entrepreneurship, hackathons, and youth leadership.

He is a student at St Mary’s CBS Portlaoise, with academic interests spanning Computer Science, Physics, Business, Accounting, and Mathematics. He is also publicly associated with Pyca Ireland and participates in student leadership and inclusion initiatives including the Student Digital Leadership Team, Schools of Sanctuary, and Treori.
